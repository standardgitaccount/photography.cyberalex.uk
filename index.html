Approach

Designed to read well in seconds but reward anyone who chooses to stay longer.

This portfolio is intentionally one page. It keeps the navigation frictionless for assessors and clients, while making it obvious how each image relates back to a clear visual logic: thirds, diagonals, and the gentle asymmetry of the golden ratio.

The short captions do the immediate work — what is this, where is it, why does it matter. The expandable write-ups go further when needed, linking individual frames back to themes of privacy, control, and movement. That dual layer of text mirrors how the images themselves work: legible at a glance, but structured enough to stand up to close reading.

  • • One-page layout with keyboard-friendly navigation.
  • • Every thumbnail opens full screen with a single click or keypress.
  • • Image text is concise by default, with optional depth on demand.
  • • Grid and hero use 1:1.618 proportions to echo photographic composition.
Built with access in mind
Accessibility notes

Semantic landmarks, alt text for all images, high contrast palette, and a lightbox that works with keyboard input and screen readers. The goal is to make the work easier to see, not harder.
